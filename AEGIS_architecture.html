AEGIS — How It Works

Proving where a product really comes from — from the factory floor to the buyer's phone

1The people

🏭Suppliers · web app

Every company in the chain signs off on its own step — what it made, where the work was done, and what it cost — using a secure digital signature.

🔗The chain

Each step is locked to the steps before it, so the whole history of a product is tamper-evident — change one link and it shows.

📱Buyers · phone app

The AEGIS Scan mobile app points the phone camera at the product's QR code and instantly shows where it's really from — no typing, no login.

↓
2The application

🛡️Security gate

Before anything is saved, AEGIS checks the signature is genuine and the supplier is registered. Anything that fails is rejected on the spot.

🧭Supplier & buyer features

Registering companies, recording steps, publishing products, and generating QR codes — all the everyday actions the apps need.

⚖️The verifier

The core service that takes a product's history and decides whether it's trustworthy and how Canadian it is.
